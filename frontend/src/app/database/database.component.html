Structure

ARGminer annotation can be downloaded as a fasta file. The annotation of each entry is reported in the fasta header as follows:

>gene_id | score:0 | validated:True/False | ARG-Class | ARG-name | ARG-mechanism | MGE-score:0 | Pat-score:0

Score: Score of the validated entry.
Validated: flag to show if the ARG has been validated.
Pat(Pathogen): Evidence of the ARG being carried by a pathogen.
MGE(Mobile Genetic Element): Evidence of the ARG being carried by a mobie element e.g., plasmids.

Genes that have not been validated conserve their original annotation (deepARG-DB)

*ARGminer uses the antibiotic resistance annotation from deepARG-DB, CARD, ARDB, UniProt, MEGARes, ACLAME, PATRIC and PubMed databases. ARGminer is free for academic use, in any othercase, please check each one of the cited resources.
